Minikatana.com

Minikatana.com specializes in handcrafted Katanas, offering a variety of miniature and full-sized swords designed with precision and rooted in Japanese tradition. Known for its quality craftsmanship, the store provides an extensive range of Katanas, from decorative pieces to functional swords. Each product showcases unique styles, blending traditional Japanese aesthetics with modern inspirations. Minikatana’s collection appeals to collectors, cosplayers, and enthusiasts alike, featuring themes inspired by popular culture and classic Japanese designs. With a strong community presence, Minikatana emphasizes quality, authenticity, and attention to detail in every piece.

SamuraiSword Store

SamuraiSword Store is dedicated to authentic Japanese sword-making, specializing in traditional Katanas, Wakizashis, and Tantos crafted by skilled artisans. Each sword is made with high-quality materials, often using high-carbon or folded steel, ensuring both durability and a sharp edge suitable for practice and collection. SamuraiSword Shop focuses on preserving the ancient techniques of Japanese forging, offering customizable options for handle, blade, and sheath styles to create a unique and personalized piece. Ideal for enthusiasts and martial artists, the store upholds the art and history of Japanese swords in each handcrafted product.
