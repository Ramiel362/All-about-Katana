About Me

Hi, I’m Rohan Vemuri, also known as Ramiel362. This website is my personal project dedicated to my passion for the art of sword fighting with Katanas. My fascination began with incredible games like Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance and Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice, which inspired me to explore the rich history and craftsmanship behind these beautiful blades.

Alongside my interest in Katanas, I’m actively pursuing and improving my skills in German, bodybuilding, sports, C++, Python, and expanding my knowledge of computers and operating systems through various online courses. If you’d like to get in touch, you can find my contact information in the third section of this website.

My interests also include gaming, online culture, and literature, particularly English and European works. I hope you enjoy exploring the world of Katanas with me!

All About Katana

Welcome to Katana no Subete!

This site is my passion project, where I dive into the awesome world of Katanas and sword fighting. It's not just a collection of facts—it's a reflection of my journey and love for these incredible blades that first caught my eye in games like Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance and Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice.

Here, you’ll find some sorts of cool insights about Katanas, from their rich history to the various families of the blades. Plus, I've included some information on training with Katanas,which can help you learn techniques and practice to hone your skills.

I created this space not just for myself but for anyone who shares this fascination or is just curious about the art of sword fighting.

As I work on my Computer Science Engineering degree with a focus on AI and Machine Learning at Lovely Professional University, I’m also leveling up my web development skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This website is a key part of that journey, helping me learn while connecting with fellow enthusiasts.

So, whether you’re here to soak up knowledge, share your own stories, or just admire the beauty of Katanas, Katana no Subete is the place for you. Let’s explore this amazing world together!
